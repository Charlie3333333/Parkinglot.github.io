Main Questions

Who is this site for?

This site is for students, faculty, staff, and visitors who drive to campus and want to find parking faster.

How do I use this?

You should check the “Live Availability” page first. Then drive to the lot that still has open spaces and please check if you are allowed to park there.

Why is the number sometimes wrong?

Sometimes the data is slow processed to the website. If you see something that is not true, please go to the Report page and tell us. Thanks!

Do I still have to follow permit rules?

Yes. There are so many parking rules on campus. You must still follow campus parking rules. This site does not replace official policy.
